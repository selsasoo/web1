Ingredients
1 tablespoon za’atar
2 pieces of naan bread
2 tablespoons DeLallo Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 (15.1-ounce) jar DeLallo Roasted Red Pepper Strips
1 English cucumber, seeded and chopped
1 (5.3-ounce) jar DeLallo Pitted Calamata Olives, drained and halved
4 ounces feta cheese
1 bunch of basil, chopped
Directions
Combine za’atar and oil in a small bowl.
Using a grill or grill pan, heat each naan until it begins to crisp and brown, about 3-5 minutes on each side. Remove from heat.
Brush za’atar oil on one side of each naan bread.
Top with peppers, cucumbers, olives, feta and basil.
Drizzle with olive oil. Slice and serve.
